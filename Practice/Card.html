Cat Category

Short-Hair

A domestic short-haired cat is a cat of mixed ancestry - thus not belonging to any particular recognized cat breed - possessing a coat of short fur.

Ragdoll

The Ragdoll is a cat breed with a color point coat and blue eyes. They are large and muscular semi-longhair cats with a soft and silky coat.

Persian

The Persian cat is a long-haired breed of cat characterized by its round face and short muzzle.

Siamese

The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cat. Derived from the Wichianmat landrace to Thailand.
